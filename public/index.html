Special Guests

Daisy Daffydilly

Daisy Daffydilly is a quacktastic entertainer with a knack for combining magic and comedy. Known as the "Ducktastic Magician," Daisy wows audiences with her quacktacular illusions, interactive tricks, and delightful sense of humor. From pulling rubber ducks out of hats to turning water into laughter, Daisy's performances are filled with surprises and giggles.

Stage 1 at 1:00pm every day

Bert, Ernie and Rubber Ducky

Ernie and Bert are world-famous best friends and comedic duo from Sesame Street. Ernie, the playful troublemaker, and Bert, the grumpy but lovable roommate, bring their iconic banter and comedic chemistry to QuackFest. They will be roaming the park from 8am until 9pm, when the fireworks start.

Find them in the park for photos and fun all day long

Quackie McQuackerson

Quackie McQuackerson is the ultimate rubber duck comedian, known for his quacky jokes and feather-brained antics. With his infectious laughter and quick wit, Quackie is guaranteed to have the audience rolling in the aisles.

Stage 2 at 3:00pm every day

Visit our Vendors!

Quacktastic Toys

Quacktastic Toys, a leading toy manufacturer, is thrilled to sponsor QuackFest. They will set up an interactive booth where festival attendees can explore their latest rubber duck collections, participate in fun toy giveaways, and even design their own custom rubber ducks. Quacktastic Toys aims to spread joy and spark creativity among festival-goers.

Quacktastic Toys

QuackBites Snack Emporium

QuackBites Snack Emporium, a local gourmet snack shop, is excited to be a part of QuackFest. They will have a dedicated food booth where festival-goers can indulge in an array of mouthwatering duck-shaped treats, such as quack-sized sandwiches, duck-shaped cookies, and quacktastic popcorn. QuackBites Snack Emporium aims to satisfy everyone's cravings while adding a tasty twist to the festival.

The Quacktastic Kitchen

We're thrilled to serve up a scrumptious array of dishes inspired by the whimsical world of ducks. Indulge in our Quacktastic Tacos bursting with bold flavors, or satisfy your sweet tooth with our Duckie Delights Desserts.

AquaQuack Cruise Lines

AquaQuack Cruise Lines, known for their quacktastic water adventures, is a proud sponsor of QuackFest. They will offer attendees the chance to win a dreamy duck-themed cruise vacation through exciting contests and games. AquaQuack Cruise Lines will also host a "Duck Deck Party" on a specially themed boat during the festival, providing a unique and memorable experience for all.
