Visit our Vendors!

Quacktastic Toys

Quacktastic Toys, a leading toy manufacturer, is thrilled to sponsor QuackFest. They will set up an interactive booth where festival attendees can explore their latest rubber duck collections, participate in fun toy giveaways, and even design their own custom rubber ducks. Quacktastic Toys aims to spread joy and spark creativity among festival-goers.

Quacktastic Toys

QuackBites Snack Emporium

QuackBites Snack Emporium, a local gourmet snack shop, is excited to be a part of QuackFest. They will have a dedicated food booth where festival-goers can indulge in an array of mouthwatering duck-shaped treats, such as quack-sized sandwiches, duck-shaped cookies, and quacktastic popcorn. QuackBites Snack Emporium aims to satisfy everyone's cravings while adding a tasty twist to the festival.

QuackBites

The Quacktastic Kitchen

We're thrilled to serve up a scrumptious array of dishes inspired by the whimsical world of ducks. From our juicy Quacker Burger topped with savory layers to our perfectly crispy Quack Fries, each bite is a flavorful adventure. Indulge in our Quacktastic Tacos bursting with bold flavors, or satisfy your sweet tooth with our Duckie Delights Desserts.

Quacktastic Kitchen

AquaQuack Cruise Lines

AquaQuack Cruise Lines, known for their quacktastic water adventures, is a proud sponsor of QuackFest. They will offer attendees the chance to win a dreamy duck-themed cruise vacation through exciting contests and games. AquaQuack Cruise Lines will also host a "Duck Deck Party" on a specially themed boat during the festival, providing a unique and memorable experience for all.
